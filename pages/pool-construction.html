Fiberglass Pool Construction

Aqua Safe's fiberglass pools combine premium build quality with a smooth, non-porous shell — faster installation, lower chemical usage and easier long-term maintenance. The epitome of elegance and aquatic bliss in your backyard.

Certified to American & European Safety Standards — 16+ years of UAE expertise.

Key Features

  • Faster installation than concrete — minimal disruption
  • Smooth gelcoat surface resists algae growth
  • Lower chemical consumption due to non-porous finish
  • Superior structural flexibility — resists ground movement
  • Available in a wide range of shapes and sizes
  • Durable finish retains colour and gloss for decades
  • Lower long-term maintenance costs
