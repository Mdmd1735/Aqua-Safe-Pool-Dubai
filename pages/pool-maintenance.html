Common Pool Problems in the UAE

Dubai and Sharjah's heat and dust create unique challenges for pool owners. Our technicians are trained to handle every UAE-specific pool condition — from green pool recovery after a sandstorm to chlorine demand spikes in summer.

Green Pool & Algae

High temperatures accelerate algae growth. We treat green pools with shock chlorination, algaecide, and a full brush-down — restoring water clarity within 24–48 hours.

pH Imbalance & Scaling

UAE's hard water causes rapid calcium scaling on tiles and equipment. We monitor pH balance and total dissolved solids every visit, preventing costly tile damage and equipment wear.

Cloudy Water

Sand, dust and bather load cloud the water. Our water testing identifies the root cause — whether it's low chlorine, filter failure or pH drift — and we fix it the same visit.

Equipment Failure

Pump and filter failures are common in UAE's summer heat. Our technicians spot early warning signs during every inspection, saving you from expensive emergency repairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should a pool be cleaned in Dubai?
In Dubai's climate, pools should ideally be serviced twice a week in summer (April–October) when heat accelerates algae and chemical loss, and weekly in cooler months. Villa pools with heavy use or surrounded by trees may need more frequent visits. A monthly maintenance contract is the most cost-effective way to keep your pool consistently clean and safe.
How much does pool maintenance cost in the UAE?
Pool maintenance in the UAE typically ranges from AED 450 to AED 1,200 per month depending on pool size, visit frequency and services included. Our Essential plan starts at AED 450/month (weekly visits) and our Pro plan at AED 800/month (twice-weekly visits with full chemical balancing). See our pricing page for full details.
What chemicals are used in pool maintenance?
We use pharmaceutical-grade pool chemicals: chlorine for sanitisation (maintained between 1–3 ppm), pH adjusters to keep the water at the ideal 7.2–7.6 range, alkalinity stabilisers, calcium hardness adjusters, and algaecide as needed. All chemicals are safely dosed and fully documented in your post-visit report.
Can you fix a green or algae-filled pool?
Yes — green pool recovery is one of our most common requests. We perform a shock treatment with high-dose chlorine, apply algaecide, brush all surfaces, vacuum the debris, and balance the water chemistry. Most pools are clear within 24–48 hours. Severe algae cases may require a second treatment visit, included at no extra charge.
Do you service pools across all UAE emirates?
Yes. Aqua Safe Pool provides residential and commercial pool maintenance across all seven UAE emirates — Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Umm Al Quwain, Fujairah and Abu Dhabi. We specialise in villa pools across premium residential areas including Mirdif, Al Warqa, Dubai Silicon Oasis, International City and Al Nahda.
