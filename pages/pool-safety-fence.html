Pool Safety Fence FAQs

Are pool safety fences legally required in the UAE?
UAE municipalities strongly recommend — and in many new developments require — pool safety barriers for all residential pools accessible to children. Dubai Municipality guidelines align with international drowning prevention standards that mandate fencing around pools in homes with young children. Our American and European certified fences meet and exceed all UAE safety requirements.
What is the best pool fence for a UAE villa?
For most UAE villas, a removable mesh fence is the most popular choice — it provides certified child safety, can be installed and removed in under an hour, and stores compactly. Families wanting a permanent solution often choose frameless glass fencing for an unobstructed view. For the highest security with maximum visibility, our aluminum fence with self-closing gate is ideal. Our team will recommend the best option during a free site visit.
Can the pool fence be removed when not needed?
Yes — our removable mesh fences are specifically designed for this. The fence clicks in and out of anchored sleeves in seconds — no tools required. They can be installed when young children or guests are present and removed for adult-only pool parties, all while maintaining their full safety certification. This is one of the key reasons they are the UAE's most popular pool safety solution.
How high does a pool safety fence need to be?
International safety standards and UAE guidelines require pool fences to be a minimum of 1.2 metres (4 feet) high — high enough that a young child cannot climb over. All Aqua Safe fence systems are installed at a minimum 1.2m height and are certified to prevent a child aged 6 and under from climbing or passing through. Our installation team confirms compliance at handover.
Do your pool fences withstand UAE's heat and humidity?
All our fence materials are UV and corrosion resistant — specifically selected for the Gulf's climate. Mesh fabric is UV-stabilised and tested to 10+ years outdoors. Aluminum profiles are powder-coated for rust resistance. Glass panels are toughened safety glass rated for temperatures up to 70°C. We offer a full warranty on materials and installation.
