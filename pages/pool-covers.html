Smart Pool Ownership

Why UAE Pool Owners Choose Covers

Reduce Debris & Cleaning

UAE dust storms and palm debris make uncovered pools dirty fast. A pool cover reduces cleaning frequency by up to 70%, cutting your monthly maintenance costs significantly.

Cut Water Evaporation

Dubai's heat evaporates up to 5cm of pool water per week. A pool cover reduces evaporation by 90–95%, saving thousands of litres monthly and lowering your water bill.

Lower Energy Costs

A covered pool retains warmth in winter and stays cooler in summer — reducing heating and chilling system runtime by 30–50% and cutting your electricity bill.

Child & Pet Safety

Solid pool covers rated for safety loads provide an additional protection barrier when the pool is not in use — complementing a pool safety fence for complete peace of mind.

Pool Cover FAQs

Which pool cover is best for hot weather in Dubai?
For Dubai's extreme summers, a PVC removable cover or automatic sliding cover works best. These reduce evaporation by up to 95% and can be specified with heat-reflective surfaces to keep the water cooler. For winter months, the same cover helps retain warmth. We recommend covers with UV stabilisation rated to withstand 50°C+ surface temperatures.
How much can a pool cover save on water and electricity?
A pool cover can save AED 300–600 per month in evaporation water losses and chemical top-ups in Dubai's summer. Energy savings from reduced pump and heating/chilling runtime add a further AED 150–400/month depending on your equipment. Most covers pay for themselves within 12–24 months of installation.
How long does a pool cover last in UAE conditions?
Quality pool covers last 7–12 years in UAE conditions when properly maintained. All Aqua Safe covers are UV-stabilised and tested to withstand Gulf temperatures. Automatic covers include motorised mechanisms warranted for 3 years. We provide annual service inspections to maximise the lifespan of your cover.
