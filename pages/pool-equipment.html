Pool Equipment FAQs

How do I know if my pool pump needs replacing?
Signs include unusually loud operation, reduced water flow, frequent tripping of the circuit breaker, or visible leaks around the pump housing. In the UAE’s summer heat, pumps run harder and fail faster — we recommend an annual inspection. Our technicians assess your pump on every maintenance visit and flag any early warning signs before a full failure occurs.
How often should a pool filter be cleaned or replaced?
Sand filters should be backwashed every 2–4 weeks and have their sand replaced every 3–5 years. Cartridge filters need cleaning every 2–6 weeks depending on usage and bather load. In the UAE, dust storms and heavy summer use increase cleaning frequency. We inspect and service your filter on every maintenance visit as standard.
Do you supply and install all pool equipment brands?
Yes — we supply and install all major pool equipment brands including Hayward, Pentair, Zodiac, Astral, and Emaux. We stock equipment suited to the UAE’s climate and voltage requirements, and all installations come with a full warranty. We can also advise on energy-efficient alternatives that reduce your electricity bill long-term.
