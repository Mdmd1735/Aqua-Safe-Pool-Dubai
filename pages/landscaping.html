Landscaping FAQs

What plants work best around a pool in the UAE?
The best poolside plants for UAE's climate are low-water, non-leaf-shedding species that won't clog your pool — Phoenix palms, Bougainvillea (away from the water edge), Agave, Strelitzia (bird of paradise), and ornamental grasses. We avoid species with invasive roots near the pool structure and select plants that are salt-tolerant for coastal properties in Sharjah and Ajman.
Can you build a pergola over my existing pool?
Yes — we regularly install pergolas and shade sails adjacent to or partially over existing pools. We ensure structural footings don't interfere with the pool shell or underground utilities, and all designs comply with local municipality setback requirements. A pergola shading your lounging area from 10am–4pm can reduce poolside temperature by 5–8°C in summer.
How long does a poolside landscaping project take?
A standard poolside landscaping project — including planting, decking and a pergola — typically takes 2–4 weeks from design approval to completion. Larger projects combining pool construction with full garden landscaping run 8–18 weeks. We provide a fixed timeline at quotation and schedule work to minimise disruption to your household.
Do you handle landscaping across all UAE emirates?
Yes — Aqua Safe's landscaping team covers all UAE emirates: Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Umm Al Quwain, Fujairah and Abu Dhabi. We have completed poolside transformations in Mirdif, Al Warqa, Dubai Silicon Oasis, Al Nahda and International City, as well as waterfront villas in Sharjah and Ajman.
