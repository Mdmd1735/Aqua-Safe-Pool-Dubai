Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Maintenance

How often should my pool be professionally maintained?
At least once a week in the UAE. The heat accelerates algae growth and chemical evaporation, so weekly visits are the minimum for a clean, safe pool year-round.
What does a standard pool maintenance visit include?
Surface skimming, brushing walls and floor, vacuuming, chemical testing and balancing, filter inspection, and a full equipment health check.
My pool has turned green — how quickly can you fix it?
A green pool can typically be restored to crystal clear within 24–48 hours with shock treatment, algaecide, and a full clean. We handle green pool recovery across all emirates.
Do you service pools in all seven UAE emirates?
Yes — we cover Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Umm Al Quwain, Fujairah, and Abu Dhabi.

Safety Fences & Covers

What safety standards do your pool fences and covers meet?
All our safety products are certified to both American (ASTM) and European (EN 13451) standards — the highest internationally recognised benchmarks for pool safety.
Are your pool safety fences removable?
Yes — our removable mesh fences can be taken down and stored when not needed, giving you full pool access while keeping children safe when required.
Which pool fence is best for my home?
It depends on your preference. Removable mesh is the most flexible. Frameless glass gives an unobstructed view. Aluminium is the most durable long-term. We offer a free consultation to help you choose.
How long does fence or cover installation take?
Most installations are completed within a single day. Larger or more complex setups may take two days maximum.

Construction & General

How long does it take to build a new pool?
A standard concrete pool typically takes 6–10 weeks from groundbreaking to completion. Fibreglass pools are faster — usually 2–3 weeks including installation.
Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?
Yes — if you are not fully satisfied after any maintenance visit, we return at no charge. No arguments, no hidden fees.
